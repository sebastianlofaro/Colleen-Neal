Colleen Neal

MS, LPC, NCC

With 27 years of experience in education, 11 of which she has practiced as a school counselor, Colleen Neal focuses her services across the lifespan. A unique person-centered approach is used for children, teens and adults, with integration of cognitive behavioral and solution focused therapeutic methods.

Colleen and her husband are parents to 2 adult daughters. She graduated from University of Houston Clear Lake in 2005 with a Master of Science in Counseling, is a Licensed Professional Counselor in the state of Texas, and holds a certification as a National Certified Counselor. Organizations of which she is a member include Texas Counseling Association and American Counseling Association.
